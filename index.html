More About Our Team :

Sadul

If personality were a movie reference, the rumor is that Kabir Singh was partially inspired by observing him in the wild. He shows up late to every lab like punctuality is a conspiracy, looking perpetually emo and like the world personally insulted him. Academically, he couldn’t tell ASEP from CP if his life depended on it, proving that intelligence and hair length are not correlated. Every committee he joins becomes a ticket-selling scheme, and half the campus attends events they didn’t want just to escape his relentless pitch. He struts around like a “leader,” yet looks like a tired extra in a post-apocalyptic emo movie, long hair still under construction. Somehow, he can talk to any baddie on campus, but the moment reality asks for competence, he’s still buffering. A glorified hype-man with commitment issues and a vending-machine addiction. Also runs the group’s unofficial Protein Distribution Yojana — regularly bringing legendary paneer rolls made by his mom. Creator of the legendary website Dehuroad Daddies

Strengths: Can charm anyone on campus, lead events, and host like a pro.
Weaknesses: Chronically late, clueless about projects, addicted to vending machines, and perpetually emo.

That’s not a commute, that’s a pilgrimage. Every morning he completes the Bus–Metro–Auto cinematic universe just to reach campus and stare respectfully from 17 feet away. By first lecture, he’s already survived three transport bosses and one existential crisis. Currently conducting long-term research on one girl from Mechanical — thesis pending, results inconclusive, eye contact minimal.

Strengths: Delusion-powered motivation, elite daydream endurance.
Weaknesses: Reality, cut-off lists, and probably push-ups. Still standing. Still simping. Nation first (after her attendance).
